## 项目详情

### Project 1: Zoomily (Pet Brand VI)
**Role:** Visual Identity Design
**Description:** Zoomily is a pet brand for which a visual identity (VI) system has been created. The color scheme is inspired by the refined colors prevalent in popular Chinese court dramas from 2018. The brand primarily targets female customers and incorporates elements from brands favored by women. A black cat character has been introduced as well to enhance the brand's recognition. The design project also includes some packaging applications.

### Project 2: Joyoung Festival Gift
**Role:** Chief Designer
**Company:** Joyoung (九阳)
**Description:** Joyoung, as a well-known brand in the small home appliances industry, distributes New Year gift packs to employees and major customers to enhance brand recognition. The inspiration for this design came from three of Joyoung's main products of 2020 - a water dispenser, a rice cooker, and a cooking machine. The visuals prominently feature Joyoung's brand symbol, the Joyoung circle, its brand color orange, classic elements like soymilk, along with some classic Chinese New Year elements.

### Project 3: Tencent Welfare Gift
**Role:** Chief Designer
**Studio:** ThinkBig Studio
**Description:** In Tencent's public welfare fundraising campaign, many enterprises have participated in creating social public welfare activities. Tencent public welfare recorded their deeds and awarded them, and summarized them in this 100-day plan as a gift for all participants and participating youth. The overall packaging is made of PVC shell and special public welfare paper, and the 100-day plan includes a to-do list and special stickers.

### Project 4: Carnivo Toy Gift
**Role:** 3D Modeling, Package Design
**Team:** Illustrator: NieNie, Peng JingTao / IP Designer: KK / Chief Designer: Haha
**Studio:** ThinkBig Studio
**Description:** For the special holiday of Children's Day, Carnival Advertising Company hopes to give each of its major clients a gift that brings back childhood innocence. By co-branding with the IP image of "little white" from ThinkBig, various gaming elements are used to represent the diverse creativity that bursts forth from play among advertisers. The gift is packaged in a transparent PVC box containing a skateboard and a roly-poly toy figure of "little white".

### Project 5: Joyoung Recruit
**Role:** Chief Designer
**Company:** Joyoung (九阳)
**Description:** Campus recruitment is a crucial initiative for major companies to recruit new talent. Despite being a renowned brand in the small home appliances industry, Joyoung is perceived by young people as a traditional, outdated brand known for soymilk machines. The challenge of this project is to visually alter the brand image of Joyoung in the minds of young people, while keeping the brand's DNA intact, and attract more young people to work at Joyoung. The final visual output chose to use large areas of orange, the brand symbol and font of Joyoung in a glass texture, and fun typography.

### Project 6: Tencent Festival Gift
**Role:** 3D Modeling
**Team:** Illustrator: NieNie, Peng JingTao / Chief Designer: Haha
**Studio:** ThinkBig Studio
**Description:** The greatest challenge for Dragon Boat Festival gift packs in the advertising industry is how to make them both creative and practical. Skateboarding is a popular sport among young people, and transforming a skateboard into a chair is both decorative and practical. Using the concept of "tide player", illustrations combining the Tencent penguin, Dragon Boat Festival elements, etc., are applied to the skateboard chair. Additionally, a carpet and triangle-shaped dumpling packaging are included, allowing the whole set to be taken out for camping or picnicking.

### Project 7: Tencent Esports Gift
**Role:** Contents (Calendar) Design, Photography
**Team:** Illustrator: Peng JingTao / Chief Designer: Haha
**Studio:** ThinkBig Studio
**Description:** The Tencent Esports New Year gift pack, styled in the form of a chip bag, contains creative items like a calendar, stickers, and more. The centerpiece of the gift pack is the creative calendar that features 365 different esports highlights, esports jokes, and industry jargon. This allows the employees who receive the gift pack to resonate with the content every day for 365 days.
